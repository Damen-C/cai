Greetings! My name is Danmeng Cai, and I am currently pursuing a Master of Science degree in Informatics at the University of Tsukuba. I specialize in Natural Language Processing and conduct my research at the Machine Learning & Language Understanding Lab. My academic interests also extend to other fields in machine learning, particularly exploring cutting-edge developments in the field. I am passionate about exploring the potential of AI to contribute to sustainable development across a range of areas, including business, education, and the environment.

I earned a Bachelor's degree in International Relations and a Master's degree in International Area Studies. I participated in the Health Economics track of the International Summer School organized by Hamburg University in July 2019. It was there that I was first exposed to the applications of machine learning in healthcare systems. Later that same year, I had the opportunity to study abroad at Washington University in St. Louis, which further fueled my passion for programming and data analysis.

Beyond academia, I have gained valuable work experience as a full-time social media account manager at a cosmetics and supplement manufacturer in the Chugoku area of Japan. In this role, I was responsible for planning and executing campaign activities on various social media platforms such as Weibo, WeChat, Redbook, and Douyin. In addition to this, I have also worked part-time as a tutor, translator, and data analyst. With fluency in Chinese, Japanese, and English at a business level, I am able to effectively communicate and collaborate with individuals from diverse backgrounds.

In my free time, I enjoy writing articles about NLP and machine learning on Medium. Feel free to check out my profile, and I'm sure you'll find something new and interesting to learn!
